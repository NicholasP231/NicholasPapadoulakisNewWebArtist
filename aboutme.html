About Me


From an early age, I have been interested in animations and cartoon characters. I like drawing
landscapes,cartoon characters, and creating stories. I make comics, political sketches, collages, artworks
from my laptop, video editings, 3D animations, and gameson my laptop.
My future goals are to become an animator and game designer and hopefully work at an animation
studio and a game company here in NYC. My influences about my artwork are everyday events and 3D
Pixar Movies like Cars (Disney Pixar) and Toy Story. From games,
my influences are Super Mario and Smash Bros Series.
Other influences are past and current events and memories of my life.
Not only do I express my emotions with my art, but I also try to pass social messages from my work.
On my part-time job, I work at my uncle's flowershop which is called
Long Island City Florist (LIC Florist). On this background image, it's one of the beautiful
beaches in Agia Marina (Chania, Crete).
